A curated collection of high-quality resources beyond individual papers — blog posts, interviews, benchmark hubs, and essential tools. Each entry includes a note on why it's worth your time.

## Blogs & Technical Writing

### Research Labs

- **[OpenAI Research](https://openai.com/research)** — Official research blog covering GPT-series, alignment, and safety work. Primary source for system cards and technical reports.
- **[Google DeepMind Blog](https://deepmind.google/research/blog/)** — Covers Gemini, robotics (RT-series), and fundamental research. High signal-to-noise ratio.
- **[Anthropic Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research)** — Constitutional AI, interpretability, and alignment-focused work.
- **[Meta AI Blog](https://ai.meta.com/blog/)** — LLaMA releases, PyTorch updates, and open-source ML research.
- **[Hugging Face Blog](https://huggingface.co/blog)** — Practical ML: model releases, RLHF tutorials, open-source tooling.

### Independent & Community

- **[Lilian Weng's Blog](https://lilianweng.github.io/)** — Exceptionally clear deep-dives on LLMs, RL, diffusion models, and agents. A canonical resource.
- **[Sebastian Ruder's NLP News](https://newsletter.ruder.io/)** — Monthly newsletter covering NLP/LLM research trends.
- **[The Gradient](https://thegradient.pub/)** — In-depth interviews and analysis on AI research, written for practitioners.
- **[Ahead of AI (Sebastian Raschka)](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/)** — Clear explanations of recent papers and methods.

## Interviews & Talks

### Landmark Interviews

- **[Lex Fridman Podcast — AI Series](https://lexfridman.com/podcast/)** — Long-form conversations with Yann LeCun, Andrej Karpathy, Sam Altman, Ilya Sutskever, and others. Essential historical record of the field's thinking.
- **[Dwarkesh Patel Podcast](https://www.dwarkeshpatel.com/podcast)** — Deep technical interviews on scaling, AGI timelines, and safety. Guests include Dario Amodei, Leopold Aschenbrenner, and key researchers.
- **[Machine Learning Street Talk](https://www.youtube.com/@MachineLearningStreetTalk)** — Rigorous academic-style discussions on AI safety, interpretability, and foundations.

### Lecture Series

- **[Stanford CS324 — Large Language Models](https://stanford-cs324.github.io/winter2022/)** — Course materials covering LLM fundamentals, scaling, and societal impact.
- **[MIT 6.S191 — Introduction to Deep Learning](http://introtodeeplearning.com/)** — Annually updated course, good baseline for all areas in this library.
- **[Pieter Abbeel: Robot Learning Lectures](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~pabbeel/)** — Foundation for understanding imitation learning and deep RL in robotics.

## Benchmarks & Leaderboards

- **[HELM](https://crfm.stanford.edu/helm/)** — Holistic Evaluation of Language Models. Tracks performance across 42 scenarios and 98 metrics.
- **[Open LLM Leaderboard (HuggingFace)](https://huggingface.co/spaces/HuggingFaceH4/open_llm_leaderboard)** — Community benchmark for open-weight LLMs across standard eval suites.
- **[Papers With Code — State of the Art](https://paperswithcode.com/sota)** — Living leaderboards across CV, NLP, Robotics. Useful for tracking benchmark progress.
- **[EvalPlus](https://evalplus.github.io/leaderboard.html)** — Rigorous code generation evaluation with augmented test cases.
- **[Open X-Embodiment Dataset](https://robotics-transformer-x.github.io/)** — The largest cross-embodiment robot learning dataset; companion to RT-X.

## Tools & Frameworks

- **[PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/)** — The primary framework for research implementations. Most papers in this library have PyTorch code.
- **[Hugging Face Transformers](https://github.com/huggingface/transformers)** — De-facto library for LLM training, fine-tuning, and inference.
- **[Diffusers](https://github.com/huggingface/diffusers)** — Hugging Face library for diffusion model pipelines (Stable Diffusion, ControlNet, etc.).
- **[LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot)** — Hugging Face library for real-world robot learning; includes Diffusion Policy and ACT implementations.
- **[lm-evaluation-harness](https://github.com/EleutherAI/lm-evaluation-harness)** — Standard framework for LLM evaluation, used in HELM and Open LLM Leaderboard.
