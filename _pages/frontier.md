FMI-Research organizes frontier machine intelligence research into four major areas. Each area page provides a structured view of key subfields, representative tasks, and curated paper groups.

---

## Research Areas

### [AGI & Large Language Models](/frontier/agi/)

Foundations of modern large language models — scaling, alignment, evaluation, and agentic reasoning systems.

**Subfields:** LLM Foundations & Scaling · Alignment, Safety & Evaluation · Agentic LLMs  
**Key topics:** Scaling laws · RLHF / DPO · Instruction tuning · Benchmarks · Tool use · Planning

---

### [Computer Vision](/frontier/cv/)

Open-world visual understanding and generative visual synthesis — from foundation perception models to diffusion-based generation.

**Subfields:** Open-world Perception · Generative Vision  
**Key topics:** Segmentation · Grounded detection · Self-supervised features · Text-to-image · 3D generation · Video synthesis

---

### [Robotics](/frontier/robotics/)

Learning-based robot manipulation and control, from imitation learning to large-scale robot foundation models.

**Subfields:** Manipulation & Imitation Learning · Foundation Models & VLA  
**Key topics:** Behavior cloning · Diffusion Policy · Robot datasets · RT-series · Vision-Language-Action models

---

### [Embodied AI](/frontier/embodied-ai/)

Agents that perceive, reason, and act in physical or simulated environments — the intersection of perception, planning, and action.

**Subfields:** Embodied Agents · World Models · Embodied Data  
**Key topics:** Embodied surveys · World model learning · Open-ended agents · Data for embodied learning

---

### [Autonomous Driving](/frontier/autonomous-driving/)

End-to-end systems, LLM/VLA-augmented driving, and world models for autonomous vehicle research.

**Subfields:** End-to-End Autonomous Driving · LLM & VLA for Driving · World Models for AD  
**Key topics:** Unified E2E architecture · Vectorized scene representation · Language-grounded planning · Chain-of-thought driving · Driving world models
